Welcome to the Signals Blog

This is the beginning of a new space where I’ll share ideas, reflections, and updates on AI, leadership, and the evolving relationship between humans and machines. Each post will build on the themes from my book and my work with organizations navigating the Copilot era.

Expect monthly updates, practical insights, and a transparent look at how I think about responsible AI adoption in real-world environments.

Why I Built This Site

After publishing my book and speaking with hundreds of leaders, it became clear that people needed a place to go for ongoing updates — not just a static text. AI moves too quickly for a book to stay current on its own.

This site is my way of keeping the conversation alive. You’ll find new chapters, speaking engagements, articles, and practical guidance for M365 Copilot power users, CIOs, CISOs, and anyone leading AI initiatives.
